Kollam (erstwhile Quilon) is a southern district of Kerala, located 70 km north of the state's capital Thiruvananthapuram. ... The heart land of Kollam is naturally veined by the Ashtamudi lake, a massive, multi-branched water body .Ashtamudi Lake located in the Kollam District of the Indian state of Kerala, is the most visited backwater and lake in the state. ... The lake is also called the gateway to the backwaters of Kerala and is well known for its houseboat and resorts.Click here to know more about kollam

Punalur Suspension Bridge

Punalur Suspension Bridge or Hanging Bridge, the ONLY ONE in Kerala and possibly in the World was built by Albert Henry, a Scottish Engineer, across the Kallada River in the year 1877.

Thirteen-Arch Railway Bridge

The 13 Kannara bridge or 13 Arch Bridge (Pathimoonu kannara palam) is a 108–year–old bridge at Kazhathuruthi. ... Sandwiched by the Kollam-Thirumangalam National Highway

Jadayu Earth Center

Jatayu Earth Center, also known as Jatayu Nature Park or Jatayu Rock, is a park and tourism centre at Chadayamangalam in Kollam district of Kerala.

Thenmala Ecotourism

Thenmala is a tourist destination in the eastern side of Kollam district in Kerala, India. The place is world-famous because of the presence of the first eco-tourism centre in India. The word 'Thenmala' means "'Honey hills" in Malayalam language.

Thangassery Light House

Tangasseri Lighthouse or Thangassery Lighthouse is situated at Tangasseri in Kollam city of the Indian state of Kerala. ... In operation since 1902, the cylindrical lighthouse tower painted with white and red oblique bands has a height of 41 metres (135 ft), making it the second tallest lighthouse in Kerala coast.
